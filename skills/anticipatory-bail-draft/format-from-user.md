## Section 4 — Grounds for Anticipatory Bail

```
GROUNDS

A. The Applicant has deep roots in the community — [permanent address, family ties, employment, profession, no flight risk].

B. The Applicant has no criminal antecedents [where applicable].

C. The Applicant will not abscond and undertakes to attend the Investigating Officer as and when required.

D. The Applicant will not tamper with evidence or influence any witness.

E. Custodial interrogation of the Applicant is not necessary, in that [the investigation material is documentary / electronic / can be procured without arrest].

F. The offences alleged against the Applicant are [bailable / triable by Magistrate / non-violent / economic] in nature [where applicable per the Satender Antil framework].

G. [Where medical / age / health ground is invoked, with supporting records annexed.]
```

## Section 5 — Undertaking paragraph

```
The Applicant undertakes:

(a) To make the Applicant available for interrogation by the Investigating Officer as and when required;

(b) Not to tamper with evidence or threaten / influence any witness;

(c) Not to leave the local jurisdiction or the country without prior permission of this Hon'ble Court;

(d) To abide by such other conditions as this Hon'ble Court may impose.
```

## Section 6 — Prayer

```
It is, therefore, prayed that this Hon'ble Court may kindly be pleased to:

(a) Grant anticipatory bail to the Applicant in connection with FIR No. [____] of [Year] registered at [Police Station Name] under Sections [____] of the BNS 2023 (corresponding to Sections [____] of the IPC 1860);

(b) Direct that in the event of arrest, the Applicant be released on bail on such conditions as this Hon'ble Court may impose;

(c) Pass any further order(s) in the interest of justice.
```
