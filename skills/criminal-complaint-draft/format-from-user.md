## Section 1 — Cause Title (Criminal Complaint)

```
IN THE COURT OF THE [JUDICIAL MAGISTRATE FIRST CLASS / CHIEF JUDICIAL MAGISTRATE per state-config.md] AT [Place], DISTRICT [District Name]

COMPLAINT CASE NO. _____ OF [YEAR]

In the matter of:
PRIVATE CRIMINAL COMPLAINT UNDER SECTION 223 OF THE BHARATIYA NAGARIK SURAKSHA SANHITA, 2023
(corresponding to Section 200 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973)
For the offences punishable under Sections [____] of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023
(corresponding to Sections [____] of the Indian Penal Code, 1860)

COMPLAINANT:-
[Name] S/o / D/o [Father's Name]
Aged about [age] years, Occupation – [occupation], Address – [full address]

...VERSUS...

ACCUSED:-
1. [Name] S/o [Father's Name], aged about [age] years, residing at [address]
2. [Other accused as applicable]
```

```
DATE AND PLACE OF OCCURRENCE

The offence alleged in this complaint was committed on <DD.MM.YYYY> at [Place], at approximately [Time], within the territorial jurisdiction of this Hon'ble Court.
```

## Section 3 — Facts constituting the offence

```
FACTS

1. That the Complainant is a [profession / status] residing at the above address. The Accused is a [profession / relationship to Complainant].

2. [Chronological narrative of the facts constituting the offence — every documentary fact ends with the inline annexure marker: "...a copy whereof is filed herewith and marked as EXHIBIT-A".]

3. That on <DD.MM.YYYY>, the Accused [specific act constituting the offence].

[Continue numbered paragraphs.]
```

## Section 4 — Ingredients of offence mapped to facts

```
INGREDIENTS OF THE OFFENCE

For the offence under Section [N] of the BNS 2023 (corresponding to Section [M] of the IPC 1860):

Ingredient 1: [element of offence — e.g., "deception" for cheating]
Mapped to facts in paragraph [N] above, where the Accused [specific factual conduct].

Ingredient 2: [element]
Mapped to facts in paragraph [N+1] above, where [specific factual conduct].

[Continue for each ingredient of each offence alleged.]
```

## Section 6 — Prayer

```
PRAYER

It is, therefore, prayed that this Hon'ble Court may kindly be pleased to:

(a) Take cognisance of the offences alleged in this complaint under Sections [____] of the BNS 2023 (corresponding to Sections [____] of the IPC 1860);

(b) Record the statement of the Complainant on solemn affirmation under Section 225 of the BNSS 2023 (corresponding to Section 202 of the CrPC 1973);

(c) Record the statements of the witnesses named in the list filed herewith;

(d) Issue process to the Accused under Section 227 of the BNSS 2023 (corresponding to Section 204 of the CrPC 1973) to answer the charge;

(e) Pass any further order(s) in the interest of justice.
```
