## Section 1 — Application heading and Cause Title

```
APPLICATION UNDER [ORDER / RULE / SECTION] OF THE CODE OF CIVIL PROCEDURE, 1908 / [Other applicable statute]
[filed in [Suit / Case Type] No. _____ of [Year]]

IN THE COURT OF THE [JUDICIAL OFFICER DESIGNATION per state-config.md] AT [Place], DISTRICT [District Name]

APPLICANT (Plaintiff / Defendant in Suit No. ____ of ____):-
[Name] ... [age] ... [occupation] ... [address]

...VERSUS...

NON-APPLICANT (Opposite party in Suit No. ____ of ____):-
[Name] ...
```

## Section 2 — Temporary Injunction three-limb paragraphs (Dalpat Kumar test)

```
PRIMA FACIE CASE:
That the Applicant has a prima facie case in the underlying suit, in that [specific factual basis demonstrating the apparent merit of the Applicant's claim — e.g., the registered Agreement to Sell dated <date>, the Plaintiff's possession of the suit property since <date>, etc.].

BALANCE OF CONVENIENCE:
That the balance of convenience favours grant of the temporary injunction sought, in that [specific factual basis — the Applicant has been in possession; the Defendant's alleged threat of dispossession would cause irreversible disruption to the Applicant's enjoyment of the suit property; etc.].

IRREPARABLE LOSS:
That in the absence of the temporary injunction sought, the Applicant will suffer loss that cannot adequately be compensated in damages, in that [specific factual basis — alienation to a third-party purchaser will defeat the Plaintiff's right of specific performance; demolition of the suit structure cannot be undone in damages; etc.].
```

## Section 3 — Sufficient-Cause paragraph (Condonation of Delay / Restoration)

```
SUFFICIENT CAUSE FOR DELAY:
That the delay of [N] days in [filing / restoring] the [proceeding] is occasioned by [specific factual basis — illness, medical records annexed as EXHIBIT-A; communication failure with the Applicant's previous counsel; etc.]. The delay is not deliberate or due to want of bona fides. The non-applicant will not be prejudiced by the condonation [where relevant].
```

## Section 4 — Prayer

```
It is, therefore, prayed that this Hon'ble Court may kindly be pleased to:

(a) [Specific relief — grant the temporary injunction sought; restore the suit / appeal; condone the delay; amend the pleadings; appoint a commission; etc.];

(b) Grant any further relief as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper in the interest of justice.
```

## Section 5 — Supporting Affidavit verification (CPC Order XIX Rule 3)

```
AFFIDAVIT

I, [Applicant Name], aged about [age] years, Occu. [occupation], R/o [address], the Applicant above-named, do hereby solemnly affirm and state on oath as under:

1. That I am the Applicant in the above-titled Application and am well-conversant with the facts of the case.

2. That the contents of the foregoing Application from paragraphs 1 to [N] are true to my personal knowledge / belief; the contents of paragraphs [N+1] to [M] are based on information received from [source] and believed by me to be true.

3. That no part of this Affidavit is false and nothing material has been concealed.

Verified at [Place] this [Day] day of [Month, Year].

                                                       ____________________
                                                              (Deponent)
```
