**Status:** Pre-populated baseline. Adapt or override per the specific pleading subtype (Amendment / Reply / Rejoinder / Particulars / Supplemental).

## Section 1 — Pleading heading

```
[For Amendment Pleading:]
AMENDMENT PLEADING UNDER ORDER VI RULE 17 OF THE CODE OF CIVIL PROCEDURE, 1908
[Filed pursuant to leave granted by this Hon'ble Court vide order dated <DD.MM.YYYY>
in [Suit / Case Type] No. _____ of [Year]]

[For Reply to Written Statement:]
PLAINTIFF'S REPLY TO THE WRITTEN STATEMENT FILED BY DEFENDANT NO. [N]

[For Rejoinder:]
REJOINDER ON BEHALF OF [Petitioner / Applicant] TO THE REPLY FILED BY [Respondent / Non-Applicant]
```

## Section 2 — Amendment Pleading body (paragraph-wise)

```
PURSUANT to the order of leave to amend dated <DD.MM.YYYY> in [Case Type] No. ____ of ____,
the Plaintiff above-named most humbly submits the following amendments to the plaint:

1. In paragraph [N] of the plaint, after the words "[existing text]", the following words shall be inserted: "[new text]".

2. After paragraph [M] of the plaint, the following new paragraph [M+1] shall be inserted: "[new paragraph content]".

3. [Continue for each amendment.]

The amendments above do not introduce any new cause of action or any claim that would otherwise be barred by limitation, in that [explanation where applicable].
```

## Section 3 — Reply / Rejoinder paragraph-wise response

```
With reference to paragraph 1 of the [Written Statement / Reply], the contents thereof are denied; the true position is that [response].

With reference to paragraph 2, the contents are admitted to the extent that [admitted facts]; denied to the extent that [denied facts].

[Continue paragraph-wise — every paragraph of the opposing pleading addressed.]
```
