# format-from-user.md — Section 351 BNSS / Section 313 CrPC Statement · canonical baseline

**Status:** Pre-populated baseline. This is a defence-coaching document — NOT a filed pleading. The Court frames the actual questions at trial.

---

## Section 1 — Header / metadata block

```
SECTION 351 BNSS / SECTION 313 CrPC STATEMENT — DEFENCE-PREPARED ANSWERS

For: [Accused Name], in [Sessions Trial / Criminal Case] No. _____ of [Year]
Pending before the Court of the [Designation per state-config.md] at [Place]

This document is the defence team's prepared answers to the questions the Court is likely to put under Section 351 of the BNSS 2023 (corresponding to Section 313 of the CrPC 1973). It is a coaching document, NOT a filed pleading. The actual statement is recorded by the Court in the accused's own words at trial. This document ensures internal consistency of the defence theory.
```

## Section 2 — Per-circumstance prepared answer format

```
CIRCUMSTANCE [N]

Source: [PW-N, deposition para X / document Exhibit-Y]
Content of adverse fact: "[verbatim content of the prosecution's evidence pointing to this circumstance]"

PREPARED ANSWER (one of):
(a) DENIAL: "It is not true that..."
(b) EXPLANATION: "The fact that... is admitted, but the inference that I committed the offence is not warranted because..."
(c) COUNTER-NARRATIVE: "The true position is that..."
(d) "I do not know" — for facts genuinely outside the accused's knowledge
(e) RIGHT TO SILENCE: "No comment — I am invoking my right against testimonial compulsion under Article 20(3) of the Constitution of India"

RATIONALE (for defence-team use only):
[Why this answer is recommended — defence-theory consistency, no widening of exposure, etc.]
```

- This document is privileged work product. Never filed; never disclosed to the prosecution.
