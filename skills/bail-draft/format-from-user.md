## Section 2 — Custody-details paragraph (LOAD-BEARING)

```
1. That the Applicant is the Accused in FIR No. [____] of [Year] registered at [Police Station Name] on <DD.MM.YYYY> for offences under Sections [____] of the BNS 2023 (corresponding to Sections [____] of the IPC 1860).

2. That the Applicant was arrested on <DD.MM.YYYY> at [Place of arrest] and has been in judicial custody at [Jail Name] since <DD.MM.YYYY>, a period of approximately [N] days / months.

3. That the investigation in the case is [complete / ongoing]; the charge-sheet has [been filed on <DD.MM.YYYY> / not yet been filed].
```

## Section 3 — Grounds for Bail

```
GROUNDS

A. The Applicant is innocent and has been falsely implicated in the case by [the complainant / co-accused / etc.].

B. The Applicant has been in custody for [N] days / months. The investigation is complete / charge-sheet is filed; the Applicant's continued custody serves no investigative purpose.

C. The Applicant has roots in the community — [permanent address, family ties, employment, no flight risk].

D. The Applicant has no criminal antecedents [where applicable].

E. The Applicant will not tamper with evidence or influence any witness; the evidence is largely documentary / electronic / sealed.

F. [Where parity argument is available:] Co-accused [Name] on similar footing has been released on bail vide order dated <DD.MM.YYYY> in [Bail Application No.] (copy annexed herewith as EXHIBIT-[N]).

G. [Where medical / age / health ground is invoked, with supporting records annexed.]

H. [Where Satender Antil framework applies — offence triable by Magistrate / non-violent / economic / etc.]
```

## Section 4 — Undertaking paragraph

```
The Applicant undertakes:

(a) To attend the Trial Court on every date of hearing without fail;

(b) Not to leave the local jurisdiction without prior permission of the Court;

(c) Not to tamper with evidence, threaten or influence any witness;

(d) To abide by such other conditions as this Hon'ble Court may impose.
```

## Section 5 — Prayer

```
It is, therefore, prayed that this Hon'ble Court may kindly be pleased to:

(a) Enlarge the Applicant on regular bail in FIR No. [____] of [Year] registered at [Police Station Name] under Sections [____] of the BNS 2023 (corresponding to Sections [____] of the IPC 1860);

(b) Impose such conditions as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit;

(c) Pass any further order(s) in the interest of justice.
```
