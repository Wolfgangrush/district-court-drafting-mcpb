# format-from-user.md — Suit for Specific Relief · canonical baseline

**Status:** Pre-populated with the validated drafting style. Adapt or override as needed. **Drafter will use this baseline if you do not customize. No fail-stop.** Bench/State-specific values come from `<case-folder>/state-config.md`.

---

## Section 1 — Cause Title

```
IN THE COURT OF THE [JUDICIAL OFFICER DESIGNATION per state-config.md] AT [Place], DISTRICT [District Name]

[SUIT TYPE — e.g., REGULAR CIVIL SUIT / SPECIAL CIVIL SUIT] NO. _____ OF [YEAR]

(In the matter of suit for [Specific Performance / Declaration / Perpetual Injunction / Mandatory Injunction]
under Sections [10/34/38/39] of the Specific Relief Act, 1963)

PLAINTIFF:-
[Name] S/o / D/o [Father's Name]
Aged about [age] years, Occupation – [occupation],
Address – [full address]

...VERSUS...

DEFENDANT:-
[Name],
Aged about [age] years, Occu. [occupation], R/o [address]
```

## Section 2 — Statement of Facts opening

```
The Plaintiff above-named most humbly and respectfully begs to submit as under:

1. That the Plaintiff is a permanent resident of the above address. The Defendant is a [profession / status] residing at the above address.

2. [Chronological narrative of the contract / cause-of-action / property transaction. Every documentary fact ends with the inline annexure marker: "...a copy whereof is filed herewith and marked as EXHIBIT-A" (or per state-config annexure prefix).]

3. That on <DD.MM.YYYY>, the Plaintiff and the Defendant entered into [Agreement to Sell / Contract / Conveyance / etc.] for [property / subject matter]. The copy of the said [Agreement / Contract] dated <DD.MM.YYYY> is filed herewith and marked as EXHIBIT-A.
```

## Section 3 — Readiness and Willingness paragraph (Section 16(c) — LOAD-BEARING)

```
That the Plaintiff has at all times been ready and willing to perform the essential terms of the [Agreement / Contract] dated <DD.MM.YYYY> on the Plaintiff's part, including [specific acts of readiness — tender of consideration, attendance at the Sub-Registry, execution of necessary documents, etc.]. The Plaintiff continues to be ready and willing to perform the Plaintiff's part of the said [Agreement / Contract]. [For monetary consideration: "The Plaintiff has tendered / is willing and able to tender the sum of ₹[amount] being the contractual consideration."]
```

## Section 4 — Jurisdiction, Limitation, Court-Fee

```
JURISDICTION
The cause of action arose at [Place], which falls within the territorial jurisdiction of this Hon'ble Court under Section 16(d) CPC [for immovable-property suits] / Section 20 CPC [for personal-action suits]. The value of the suit being ₹[Value] falls within the pecuniary jurisdiction of this Hon'ble Court as fixed by the [State Civil Courts Act per state-config.md].

LIMITATION
The cause of action for the present suit arose on <DD.MM.YYYY>. The present suit is filed on <DD.MM.YYYY>, well within the period of three years prescribed by Article 54 [for Specific Performance] / Article 58 [for Declaration] / Article 113 [for Injunction] of the Schedule to the Limitation Act, 1963.

COURT-FEE
The value of the suit for the purpose of court-fee and jurisdiction is ₹[Value]. The court-fee payable thereon is ₹[Court-Fee Amount] paid under [Schedule citation per state-config.md].
```

## Section 5 — Prayer

```
PRAYER

In the premises aforesaid, it is most respectfully prayed that this Hon'ble Court may kindly be pleased to:

(a) Pass a decree of specific performance directing the Defendant to execute and register the [Sale Deed / Conveyance / instrument] in favour of the Plaintiff in pursuance of the [Agreement / Contract] dated <DD.MM.YYYY>;

(b) In the alternative, pass a decree directing the Defendant to refund the sum of ₹[amount] paid by the Plaintiff together with interest at [rate] from <DD.MM.YYYY> and damages of ₹[amount] under Section 22 of the Specific Relief Act, 1963;

(c) Pass a decree for perpetual injunction restraining the Defendant from alienating / encumbering / parting with possession of the suit property pending the disposal of the suit;

(d) Award the costs of the suit;

(e) Pass any further order(s) as this Hon'ble Court may kindly be pleased to grant in the facts and circumstances of the case and in the interest of justice.
```

## Section 6 — Verification (CPC Order VI Rule 15, verbatim)

```
VERIFICATION

I, [Plaintiff Name], the Plaintiff above-named, do hereby verify on solemn affirmation that the contents of the foregoing paragraphs 1 to [N] of the plaint are true to my personal knowledge / belief, and the contents of paragraphs [N+1] to [M] are true to the best of my information received from [source] and believed by me to be true.

Verified at [Place] this [Day] day of [Month, Year].

                                                       ____________________
                                                              (Plaintiff)
```
