## Section 1 — Cause Title (Criminal Revision)

```
IN THE COURT OF THE [SESSIONS JUDGE per state-config.md] AT [Place], DISTRICT [District Name]

CRIMINAL REVISION APPLICATION NO. _____ OF [YEAR]
(Arising out of order dated <DD.MM.YYYY> passed by the learned [Subordinate Court Designation]
at [Place] in [Case Type & Number])

APPLICANT (Accused / Complainant / Aggrieved party):-
[Name] ... [age] ... [occupation] ... [address]

...VERSUS...

NON-APPLICANT(S):-
1. The State of [State] through the Police Station Officer, [Police Station Name], [District]
2. [Other parties as applicable]
```

## Section 2 — Impugned-Order paragraph

```
1. That by the impugned order dated <DD.MM.YYYY> passed by the learned [Subordinate Court Designation] at [Place] in [Case Type & Number], the learned [Court] [briefly state the impugned outcome — convicted / sentenced / framed charges / rejected discharge / etc.]. A certified copy of the impugned order is annexed herewith and marked as EXHIBIT-A.

2. That the impugned order is NOT an interlocutory order within the meaning of Section 438(2) of the BNSS 2023 (corresponding to Section 397(2) of the CrPC 1973), and revision lies against the said order before this Hon'ble Court under Section 438 read with Section 442 of the BNSS 2023 (corresponding to Sections 397 and 401 of the CrPC 1973).
```

## Section 3 — Facts chronology + Grounds for Revision

```
FACTS

[Brief chronology of the underlying criminal proceeding leading to the impugned order. Each paragraph numbered.]

GROUNDS

A. The learned [Subordinate Court] exercised jurisdiction not vested by law in passing the impugned order, in that [specific jurisdictional defect].

B. The learned [Subordinate Court] acted illegally / with material irregularity in [specific procedural error].

C. The impugned order is contrary to the settled position of law that [legal proposition], as laid down in [authority — if traced to user's citations.md].

D. The impugned order is contrary to the evidence on record in that [specific evidentiary disconnect].

E. The impugned order results in manifest injustice / abuse of process in that [specific harm].

[Note: Revision is NOT an appeal. Each ground identifies a specific error of jurisdiction / illegality / irregularity / impropriety — not a merits re-argument.]
```

## Section 4 — Prayer

```
It is, therefore, prayed that this Hon'ble Court may kindly be pleased to:

(a) Call for the record of [Case Type & Number] from the Court of the learned [Subordinate Court Designation] at [Place];

(b) Set aside / modify / reverse the impugned order dated <DD.MM.YYYY> passed by the learned [Subordinate Court] in [Case Type & Number];

(c) Pending hearing and final disposal of the present Revision Application, stay the operation of the impugned order;

(d) Pass any further order(s) as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper in the interest of justice.
```
